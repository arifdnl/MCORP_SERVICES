Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a performing arts centre in Melaka that is recognised at the state level, serving as a pioneer in Bangsawan theatre and the preservation of Malay cultural heritage.

Our Mission

  • To preserve and promote traditional Malay arts through high-quality and professionally produced performances.
  • To provide a platform for local talents to create, develop, and showcase their artistic potential.
  • To contribute to the growth of the tourism industry by offering distinctive and meaningful cultural attractions.
